üç∑ Douro Table Wine Styles

Red Douro DOP:

Historically Douro focused on Port, but since 1980s quality dry wines increasingly important and gaining international recognition.

Bairrada DOP

üìç Location

Coastal region south of Porto, Atlantic influence

üå§Ô∏è Climate

Moderate maritime, wet, cooler than interior regions

ü™® Soil

Clay and limestone

üçá Key Grape

Baga: Thick-skinned, late-ripening red variety. High tannin, high acidity, needs warm sites to ripen fully. Can be very astringent when young but softens with age.

üç∑ Wine Styles

Traditional Baga: Very high tannin, high acidity, tough when young, needs extended aging (10+ years) to soften.

Modern Baga: Better site selection, crop thinning, oak aging. More approachable young, still structured. Black fruit, herbal notes.

Sparkling wines: Bairrada also produces traditional method sparkling wines (high acidity ideal).
