The region of Tokaj (note: the wine is called Tokaji) is located in the foothills of the Carpathian Mountains in the far northeast of Hungary, close to the Slovakian border. Tokaj has a long-standing reputation for producing premium botrytised sweet wines, though Late Harvest sweet wines and dry wines are becoming more popular.
