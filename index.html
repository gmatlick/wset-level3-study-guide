Welcome to Your Path to Distinction

Top Tips for Distinction

Be Specific: Don't just say "Bordeaux" - specify Left Bank vs Right Bank, specific appellations, and grape varieties.
Explain Why: The exam rewards understanding of cause and effect. Always connect factors to their impact on style, quality, and price.
Use Technical Language: Use proper terminology (e.g., "v√©raison", "malolactic conversion", "autolysis") where appropriate.
Practice Writing: Short answer questions require clear, structured responses. Practice writing 25-mark answers in 30 minutes.
Taste Systematically: Use the WSET SAT for every wine. Build muscle memory so you can complete it accurately under exam pressure.
