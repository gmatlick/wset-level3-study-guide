What to Expect

Examination Structure

Two units testing theory and practical tasting ability

Unit 1

Theory Examination

2 hours

Part 1: Multiple Choice

  • 50 questions (1 mark each)
  • 55% required to pass
  • Tests knowledge across all learning outcomes

Part 2: Short Written Answers

  • 4 questions (25 marks each)
  • 100 marks total
  • 55% required to pass
  • Tests application of knowledge
Strategy: Allow 2.4 minutes per MCQ, 30 minutes per written answer
Unit 2

Tasting Examination

30 minutes

Blind Tasting

  • 2 still wines (1 white, 1 red)
  • 41 marks total
  • 55% required to pass
  • WSET Level 3 Systematic Approach

Assessment Criteria

  • Accurate description of appearance, nose, palate
  • Quality assessment
  • Readiness for drinking evaluation
  • Precision in technical terminology
Strategy: Practice the SAT daily until it becomes automatic

Grading Scale

Distinction
80%+ (no paper below 65%)

65-79%
