California

Napa Valley AVA

Climate: Warm to hot, maritime influence decreases inland, significant diurnal range

Key Grapes: Cabernet Sauvignon (king), Merlot, Cabernet Franc, Chardonnay

Style: Full body, high alcohol (14-15.5%), ripe black fruit, new French oak (vanilla, toast, spice), concentrated, age-worthy (10-30 years)

Sub-AVAs: Rutherford (dusty tannins), Oakville, Stags Leap District, Howell Mountain (elevation, structure), Mount Veeder, Los Carneros (cooler, Pinot/Chard), Saint Helena, Calistoga

Sonoma County AVA

Climate: More maritime influence, cooler overall than Napa, diverse microclimates

Russian River Valley AVA: Cool, fog, Pinot Noir & Chardonnay. Elegant, high acid, red fruit (Pinot), citrus/stone fruit (Chard)

Alexander Valley AVA: Warmer, Cabernet Sauvignon, full body, ripe fruit

Dry Creek Valley AVA: Zinfandel specialist, old vines, full body, jammy fruit, high alcohol

Sonoma Coast AVA: Very cool, coastal fog, Pinot Noir, Chardonnay, elegant, restrained

Central Coast Regions

Santa Cruz Mountains: High altitude, Pinot Noir, Cabernet, elegant, age-worthy

Monterey AVA: Cool, windy, Chardonnay, Pinot Noir, Riesling. High acidity, fresh fruit.

Oregon

Willamette Valley AVA

Climate: Cool, maritime, Burgundy-like, moderate rainfall

Grape: Pinot Noir (dominant), Chardonnay, Pinot Gris

Style (Pinot Noir): Medium body, elegant, high acidity, red fruit (cherry, raspberry), earthy, mushroom. Less oaky than California. Age 5-15 years.

Quality: World-class Pinot Noir, comparable to Burgundy

Washington State

Columbia Valley AVA

Climate: Continental, rain shadow of Cascades, hot days, cool nights, irrigation essential

Key Grapes: Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Syrah, Riesling, Chardonnay

Style: Full body, ripe fruit, good acidity (diurnal range), balance, concentrated

Yakima Valley AVA: Within Columbia Valley, warmer, Cabernet focus

New York State

Finger Lakes AVA

Climate: Cool continental, lake moderation, long growing season

Speciality: Riesling (dry to sweet, including ice wine). High acidity, citrus, stone fruit, mineral. World-class quality.
